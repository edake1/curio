## The Name

### Why Non-English?

English names feel utilitarian. We want something that:
- Carries meaning beyond the word itself
- Sounds beautiful when spoken
- Transcends a single culture
- Feels ancient yet timeless

### Final Name: **HIKMAH**

**Origin:** Arabic (حكمة)
**Pronunciation:** HIK-mah
**Meaning:** Wisdom, profound insight, the ability to apply knowledge with justice and compassion

**Why It Works:**
- Found in Arabic, Hebrew (Chokmah), and shared across Abrahamic traditions
- Sounds soft, warm, welcoming
- Short, memorable, easy to pronounce globally
- The "H" gives it breath, life
- Means not just knowledge, but *applied* wisdom — wisdom in action

## Brand Essence

### Mission
To bring the world's wisdom into daily life — one saying, one reflection, one person at a time.

### Vision
A world where ancient wisdom guides modern decisions, where every person has access to humanity's collective insight, and where reflection is as habitual as checking the weather.

### Tone
- **Warm** — Like a wise elder speaking to you directly
- **Rich** — Deep context, not surface-level quotes
- **Respectful** — Honoring each culture's contribution
- **Inviting** — Never preachy or superior
- **Timeless** — Ancient wisdom, beautifully presented

### Voice
"You're not here to be told what to think. You're here to discover what you already know."

## Comment Philosophy

### We WANT Disagreement

The app is not about passive consumption. It's about active engagement:
- "This doesn't resonate with me because..."
- "In my culture, we have a different view..."
- "I think this is outdated because..."
- "This actually contradicts another saying from..."

### Comment Guidelines
- No likes, no upvotes — every voice equal
- Disagreement welcome, disrespect is not
- Share your perspective, not your credentials
- Listen as much as you speak

### Comment Features
- Anonymous by default (country shown only)
- Threaded replies for deeper conversations
- Flag inappropriate content (human review)
- "Mark as insightful" (private bookmark, no public count)
- Share specific comments externally

## Content Strategy

### Source Categories

| Category | Examples | Quantity Goal |
|----------|----------|---------------|
| **Indigenous Wisdom** | Maori, Inuit, Aboriginal, Navajo | 2,000+ |
| **Religious Texts** | Bible, Quran, Gita, Dhammapada, Torah | 3,000+ |
| **Philosophical Traditions** | Stoicism, Confucianism, Zen, Existentialism | 2,000+ |
| **Folk Sayings** | Every country represented | 3,000+ |
| **Contemporary Wisdom** | Modern thinkers (verified) | 500+ |

### Quality Standards

Every saying must have:
1. ✅ Verified origin (not misattributed)
2. ✅ Cultural context written by someone from that culture OR verified by cultural consultant
3. ✅ Original language + accurate translation
4. ✅ Not already in app (deduplication)

### Cultural Consultants Network
- Partner with cultural organizations
- Pay consultants from each represented culture
- Annual review of content for accuracy
- User flagging system for corrections
